Reasonable measures

When designing the apartments of the Grand Victoria residential complex, the principle of "Reasonable meters" was used, which allowed effectively use every square meter of the area. All layouts are functional - there are no overly long ones. corridors, dead ends, and dark corners. Rational, ergonomic and thoughtful layouts allow you to functionally place furniture and make a space comfortable

Construction technology
